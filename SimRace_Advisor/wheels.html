Wheel & Base Guide

Understand drive types (gear, belt, direct drive), torque, and platform compatibility. Pick a base that matches your driver level and budget.

Drive Types

  • Gear: affordable, crisp center, more noise.
  • Belt: smoother feel, quieter, mid-tier torque.
  • Direct Drive: strongest, most detailed, needs a sturdy rig.
