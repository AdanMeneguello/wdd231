Site Plan — SimRace Advisor

1. Site Name

SimRace Advisor — Equipment Guide by Driver Level

The name directly communicates the purpose: advising sim racers on gear choices according to their skill level.

2. Site Purpose

The site helps sim racers discover their level (Beginner, Amateur, Experienced, Professional) and receive setup recommendations based on goals, budget, and platform.

  • Page 1 – Evaluation & Recommendation: driver form, level calculation, and dynamic recommendations.
  • Page 2 – Wheel & Base Guide: belt/gear/direct drive, torque, compatibility, ecosystems (Logitech, Moza, Fanatec, etc.).
  • Page 3 – Games & Platforms: overview of sim titles, requirements, and platforms (PC/console/VR).

3. Scenarios

  1. “I have $400 and race on PlayStation — what’s the best beginner setup?”
  2. “I race 8–10h per week in GT3 leagues — should I upgrade to direct drive or just pedals?”
  3. “What PC specs do I need for ACC and F1 with VR?”
